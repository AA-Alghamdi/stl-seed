# stl-seed — abstract

Soft-verified SFT — accepting trajectories with a continuous proxy reward instead of a binary pass/fail — is the dominant recipe for instructing small open-weights LLMs on verifiable tasks. The soft signal is almost always *constructed* (line-overlap on a patch, learned RM, judge-LLM rubric), so the Goodhart decomposition $R\_\\text{gold} - R\_\\text{proxy} = (R\_\\text{gold} - R\_\\text{spec}) + (R\_\\text{spec} - R\_\\text{verifier})$ stays unauditable: spec-completeness and verifier-fidelity error terms are entangled in the verifier's training residual.

stl-seed is a SERA-style recipe (Shen et al. 2026, arXiv:2601.20789) where the soft signal is mathematically primitive: Signal Temporal Logic robustness $\\rho(\\tau, \\varphi)$ (Donzé-Maler 2010) on simulated trajectories from a closed-form ODE. Because $R\_\\text{verifier}$ is *defined* as the recursive Donzé-Maler evaluator on the same $(\\tau, \\varphi)$ that $R\_\\text{spec}$ is defined over, the verifier-fidelity term collapses to a float64 floor ($\\le 10^{-6}$ after $\\sigma$-squashing). The whole gap becomes the spec-completeness term. We measure it with a trajectory adversary; on `glucose_insulin.tir.easy` it is $-2.27$ ρ units against a literature-cited gold scorer.

The training recipe is one epoch of weighted-NLL SFT over a {random, heuristic, small-LLM} mixture-policy reference, on QLoRA adapters of Qwen3-{0.6B, 1.7B, 4B} bases, with three filter conditions {hard ($\\rho > 0$), top-quartile, $\\rho$-weighted softmax}. The canonical sweep is a $3 \\times 3 \\times 2$ grid (model size × filter × {bio_ode, glucose_insulin}) with 5 seeds × 25 instances × 8 BoN budgets per cell — 36,000 trials, ~$25 of RunPod 4090. Pre-registered: H1 (TOST equivalence at $\\Delta = 0.05$), H2 (size-monotone improvement), H3 (spec-completeness against a learned-critic baseline).

A pre-Phase-2 MLX QLoRA smoke on Qwen3-0.6B-bf16 drove training loss 1.484 → 0.466 monotonically in 15 s on M5 Pro, hit 5/5 held-out parse-success, produced a 4.6 MiB adapter. Empirical pilot ICC of $\\rho$ across task families is 0.9979; the hierarchical pooled SE on the global TOST contrast is 0.0098 against the registered $\\Delta = 0.05$ threshold of 0.0171, so the locked design is powered. 480+ unit tests pass plus platform-skips and documented expected-fails at 91% line coverage.

The honest failure mode is that soft significantly underperforms hard in at least one cell — a negative result on SERA's central conjecture, in the direction the literature has not yet measured.
