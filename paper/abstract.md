# stl-seed ŌĆö abstract

Soft-verified SFT, accepting trajectories with a continuous proxy reward instead of a binary pass/fail, is the dominant recipe for instructing small open-weights LLMs on verifiable tasks. The soft signal is almost always *constructed* (line-overlap on a patch, learned RM, judge-LLM rubric), so the Goodhart decomposition $R\_\\text{gold} - R\_\\text{proxy} = (R\_\\text{gold} - R\_\\text{spec}) + (R\_\\text{spec} - R\_\\text{verifier})$ stays unauditable: spec-completeness and verifier-fidelity error terms are entangled in the verifier's training residual.

stl-seed is a SERA-style recipe (Shen et al. 2026, arXiv:2601.20789) where the soft signal is mathematically primitive: Signal Temporal Logic robustness $\\rho(\\tau, \\varphi)$ (Donz├®-Maler 2010) on simulated trajectories from a closed-form ODE. Because $R\_\\text{verifier}$ is *defined* as the recursive Donz├®-Maler evaluator on the same $(\\tau, \\varphi)$ that $R\_\\text{spec}$ is defined over, the verifier-fidelity term is dominated by float64 round-off through the evaluator's min/max depth (depth $\\le 12$ here, $\\le 12$ ulp accumulation per call); empirically the held-out floor is $\\le 10^{-6}$ after $\\sigma$-squashing. This is an empirical bound, not a proof of zero. The whole interpretable gap becomes the spec-completeness term, which we measure with a trajectory adversary: on `glucose_insulin.tir.easy` it is $-2.27$ Žü units against a composite gold scorer with literature-cited components (TIR coverage from ADA / Battelino targets, an L2 jerk penalty, a glucose-variance penalty) and **dimensional-analysis blend weights set by the author**. The number is therefore an existence-style lower bound under those weights, not a population mean against an external oracle.

**Phase 1 result (shipped, falsified against a real LLM prior).** Differentiable STL-robustness as inference-time guidance, biasing the LLM logits at each generation step by $\\nabla\_{u_t} \\rho$ propagated through the Diffrax simulator and the Donz├®-Maler evaluator. On `glucose_insulin.tir.easy` (smooth dynamics) gradient guidance saturates the spec ceiling at every seed, deterministic; the contribution over wall-matched continuous-BoN is reaching the ceiling reliably rather than the +7.88$\\times$ headline lift. On the four narrow-attractor specs (repressilator, toggle, MAPK, FitzHugh-Nagumo cardiac) gradient guidance fails because 1-step lookahead is uninformative; **beam-search warmstart** over a $k\_\\text{per dim} = 5$ vocabulary scored under model-predictive constant-extrapolation lookahead is the rescue, with the standing critique that the satisfying corner is in the vocabulary by construction (transparent in code and paper). Pre-registered falsification on real `Qwen3-0.6B-bf16` (3 fixed seeds ├Ś 4 hard tasks, `paper/real_llm_comparison.md`): `Standard + Qwen3-0.6B` fails on a majority of seeds across all 4 tasks (`Žü╠ä Ōłł {ŌłÆ247.6, ŌłÆ100.0, ŌłÆ0.5, ŌłÆ1.4}`); beam-search warmstart rescues all 4 (`Žü╠ä Ōłł {+25.0, +30.0, +0.002, +0.85}`, 12/12 sat). The pre-registered outcome rule fires `METHODOLOGY MATTERS`: the inference-time methodology gap is not a flat-prior-LLM artefact.

**Phase 2 (pre-registered, queued on $25 of RunPod 4090 spot).** Training-time SERA-mimic: one epoch of weighted-NLL QLoRA SFT over a {random, heuristic, small-LLM} mixture-policy reference on Qwen3-{0.6B, 1.7B, 4B} bases ├Ś three filter conditions {hard ($\\rho > 0$), top-quartile, $\\rho$-weighted softmax} ├Ś two task families {bio_ode, glucose_insulin} = 18 cells. Pre-registered hypotheses H1 (TOST equivalence of soft and hard at $\\Delta = 0.05$), H2 (size-monotone improvement), H3 (spec-completeness against a learned-critic baseline). The mock-backend dry-run validates the full pipeline locally; an MLX QLoRA pilot smoke on `Qwen3-0.6B-bf16` drove training loss $1.484 \\to 0.466$ in 15 s on M5 Pro (5/5 held-out parse-success, 4.6 MiB adapter). The canonical sweep launches with `python scripts/run_canonical_sweep.py --confirm` and is gated only on RunPod credit.

**Power.** Empirical pilot ICC of $\\rho$ across task families is 0.9979; the hierarchical pooled SE on the global TOST contrast is 0.0098 against the registered $\\Delta = 0.05$ threshold of 0.0171, so the locked design is powered. 426+ unit tests pass plus platform-skips and documented expected-fails at 91% line coverage.

**Honest failure mode.** Phase 2 is unrun. If, when run, soft significantly underperforms hard in at least one cell, that is a negative result on SERA's central conjecture in the direction the literature has not yet measured. If soft matches or beats hard, that is the H1 contribution. Phase 1's inference-time finding stands either way.
